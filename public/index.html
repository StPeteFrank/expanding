Extended Travel

Ever wonder how you can travel for an extended period of time?

Here is an account of how we did it. If you follow these steps, you might find yourself traveling abroad for up to a year!

  • Choose your destinations.

  • Choose your dates per city.

  • Research costs and budget per city.

  • Tally up the budget needed for total number of destinations.

  • Start saving your money towards your goal.

  • Apply for travel rewards credit cards and interest free cards.

  • Set aside a reserve account for your return.
